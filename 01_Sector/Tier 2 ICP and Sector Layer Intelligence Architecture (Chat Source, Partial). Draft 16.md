## Tier 2 ICP: Post-Seed → Series A ($1M–$10M ARR)

**Why this tier:** founders are drowning (wearing all hats), investors demand predictable growth metrics, no sales playbook/SOPs/CRM hygiene exists, and they can't afford $100K+/mo retainers but can offer equity upside.

**Filter criteria:** funding stage post-Seed/Series A (or profitable enough to skip funding) · ARR $1M-$10M · team 10-50 people · 0-3 sales hires (founder still selling) · CRM exists but is messy, or doesn't exist · no VP Sales/RevOps/dedicated SDR team · growth 10-30% MoM or 100-300% YoY · board meetings require clean metrics.

**Deal structure note (illustrative, not yet a confirmed agency policy):** the source material explicitly proposes equity-based deals for this tier — e.g. "3-month engagement at 50% cash retainer + 0.5-1% equity or revenue share." This is strategic reasoning from the chat, not a documented agency offer yet — flag for Offer (02) department reconciliation if adopted.

**Sub-segment: AI-Native / Applied AI startups** — companies that build AI products but have zero sales infrastructure themselves ("the irony"). Pitch angle proposed: *"You've built an incredible AI product. But without sales infrastructure, you're leaving 70% of your TAM on the table."*

## The Client Journey for Tier 2 (4 phases)

1. **Discovery & Shock (Week 1-2):** Revenue Audit (map their entire revenue stack) → Gap Heatmap (visual dashboard, e.g. "you have 4 lead sources but only track 1") → Quick Win Extraction (3 fixable-in-7-days items). Output: a 1-page "Revenue Infrastructure Score" (0-100).
2. **Infrastructure Build (Month 1-3):** CRM architecture, pipeline design, attribution system, forecasting engine, sales playbook — built *with* the client, not just *for* them, so they understand the system.
3. **Enablement & Activation (Month 3-6):** sales training for founders/early team, content-to-sales bridge, revenue cadence (weekly pipeline reviews), AI sales assistant (automated follow-ups, lead scoring, next-best-action).
4. **Growth & Scaling (Month 6+):** channel optimization, ICP refinement from closed-won data, scale playbook, board-ready investor-grade reporting.

**Named calendar/event programs proposed** (not yet built): "Revenue Infrastructure Audit" (1:1, 45 min diagnostic), "Fix Your Pipeline Workshop" (group, 90 min), "Investor-Ready Revenue Sprint" (2-week cohort). Illustrative pricing path discussed: Free/$500 scorecard → $2.5K-$5K pipeline sprint → $15K-$25K infrastructure build → $8K-$15K/mo + equity ongoing partnership. **All pricing here is strategic brainstorm, not a confirmed agency rate card** — cross-reference Offer (02)'s existing pricing conflict (`OWNER_INPUT_NEEDED.md` item 7) before treating any of these numbers as real.
