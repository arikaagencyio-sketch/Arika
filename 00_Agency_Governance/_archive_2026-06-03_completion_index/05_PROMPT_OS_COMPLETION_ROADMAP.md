## Phase Priority Logic

Do not automate before source truth, ownership, governance, handoff contracts, and validation exist. Do not scale client execution before Client, Offer, Sales, Finance, and Operations handoffs are accepted. Do not archive or merge drafts before source IDs preserve lineage.
