**Community Narrative**

The decentralized social story people collectively construct around a brand, idea, creator, or movement.

Unlike brand narrative (controlled by the company), community narrative is socially co-authored.

It answers:

  * “What does being part of this mean?”
  * “Who are we together?”
  * “What do outsiders not understand?”
  * “What identity do we share?”



When strong, the audience stops consuming and starts participating.

**Core Function**

Community narrative transforms:

  * Customers → members
  * Followers → believers
  * Users → evangelists
  * Audience → culture



At this stage:

  * The brand no longer carries the full marketing load.
  * The community becomes the distribution engine.
  * Social reinforcement replaces paid persuasion.
