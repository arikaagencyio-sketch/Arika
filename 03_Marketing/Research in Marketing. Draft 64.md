At a high level, research answers only 4 questions:

**3\. RESEARCH METHODS (WHAT ACTUALLY WORKS)**

Forget generic surveys unless you know what you’re doing. High-performing teams use:

**1\. Qualitative (WHY)**

  * Deep interviews 
  * Voice-of-customer mining 
  * Sales transcripts 



→ This is where the **money insights** come from

**2\. Quantitative (WHAT + HOW MUCH)**

  * Analytics (traffic, conversion) 
  * Cohort analysis 
  * Funnel metrics 



→ This validates scale

**3\. Behavioral (WHAT PEOPLE DO)**

  * Click tracking 
  * Scroll tracking 
  * Purchase behavior 



→ Most accurate layer

**4\. Experimental (TRUTH)**

  * A/B testing 
  * Offer testing 
  * Pricing tests 



→ Only truth that matters = what converts

**4\. THE RESEARCH → EXECUTION LOOP (CRITICAL)**

Most people fail here. Research must feed execution continuously.

**Loop:**

  1. Hypothesis (based on research) 
  2. Launch (campaign / content / offer) 
  3. Measure (conversion, CAC, LTV) 
  4. Learn (what worked / didn’t) 
  5. Refine 



→ Repeat endlessly (this is your growth engine)
