Generated: 2026-05-19

## 1. Purpose
Operationalize who does what, when, and why across daily to yearly cycles.

## 2. Daily Cadence (Execution Engine)
## Objectives
1. Protect campaign performance
2. Detect anomalies early
3. Keep lead and funnel movement continuous

## Owner Map
1. Paid Media Systems Engineer: monitors spend, CPC, CPA, CTR, pacing
2. Funnel Architect: monitors stage conversion movement and friction alerts
3. DM Marketing Conversion Agent: monitors conversation-to-booking flow
4. Email Lifecycle Delivery Agent: monitors trigger sends and deliverability
5. Tracking Systems Architect: validates event integrity and data freshness
6. Operations Cadence Controller: conducts daily command checkpoint

## Daily Decisions
1. Micro-bid changes
2. Creative rotation decisions
3. Lead routing corrections
4. Alert escalations (if thresholds breached)

## 3. Weekly Cadence (Optimization Engine)
## Objectives

3. Refresh creative and messaging where needed

3. Experimentation Program Manager: test board and rulings
4. Attribution and Modeling Architect: channel effectiveness signals
5. Chief Marketing Strategist: weekly tactical alignment

## Weekly Decisions
1. Scale, kill, iterate test decisions
2. Channel budget reallocation
3. Offer and CTA adjustments
4. Editorial and narrative next-week priorities
