# Governance System

Governance protects continuity, meaning, trust, and operational embodiment.

## Audit Domains

- narrative continuity
- visual continuity
- emotional consistency
- cultural alignment
- psychographic alignment
- operational embodiment
- trust signaling
- symbolic coherence
- legal and compliance sensitivity
- platform adaptation

## Mandatory Output Reasoning

Client-facing outputs must explain:

- strategic reasoning
- emotional reasoning
- symbolic reasoning
- visual reasoning
- psychological reasoning
- cultural reasoning
- positioning rationale
- operational implications

## Approval Gate

An output should not ship if:

- retrieval was incomplete
- no specialist agents were activated
- the brand promise contradicts operational delivery
- cultural meaning is untested
- sales and marketing systems diverge from positioning
- visual expression conflicts with symbolic intent
- narrative claims lack proof
