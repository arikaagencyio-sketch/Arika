# Trigger-Based Agent Activation

The Master Orchestrator activates specialist agents according to task context.

## Sector Triggers

- `fintech`: trust authority, governance
- `healthcare`: trust authority, cultural intelligence, governance
- `luxury`: symbolic systems, visual cognition, psychographic intelligence
- `laundry`: sector intelligence, audience cognition, distribution intelligence
- `b2b`: trust authority, sales alignment, presentation intelligence

## Deliverable Triggers

- `visual_identity`: visual cognition, symbolic systems, governance
- `campaign`: marketing synchronization, narrative engineering, production
- `launch_campaign`: marketing synchronization, distribution intelligence, brand calendar
- `presentation`: presentation intelligence, trust authority, governance
- `sales_system`: sales alignment, trust authority, semantic intelligence

## Geography Triggers

- `nairobi`: cultural intelligence, semantic intelligence
- `kenya`: cultural intelligence, distribution intelligence
- `multi_market`: cultural intelligence, brand evolution, trend intelligence

## Audience Triggers

- `professionals`: psychographic intelligence, trust authority
- `enterprise`: trust authority, presentation intelligence, governance
- `founders`: audience cognition, sales alignment, narrative engineering

## Routing Behavior

The orchestrator always includes the base cognition chain:

- sector intelligence
- audience cognition
- cultural intelligence
- brand identity
- narrative engineering
- visual cognition
- brand governance

It then adds trigger-matched specialists and removes duplicates.
