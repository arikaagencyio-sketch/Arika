# Brand Grading System

BOIS grades brand cognition and execution across 15 dimensions.

## Dimensions

- Identity Strength: uniqueness, recognizability, clarity, symbolic strength.
- Narrative Coherence: story logic, continuity, semantic alignment, message hierarchy.
- Symbolic Consistency: semiotic fit, category codes, archetypal alignment, meaning transfer.
- Visual Intelligence: symbolism, emotional effect, platform adaptability, identity retention.
- Emotional Resonance: felt relevance, aspiration, anxiety resolution, empathy.
- Trust Signaling: proof, authority, risk reduction, consistency.
- Cultural Alignment: regional adaptation, language nuance, generational fit, symbolic interpretation.
- Psychographic Alignment: values, identity aspiration, motivation, behavioral fit.
- Sales Synchronization: offer clarity, objection handling, conversion logic, sales narrative.
- Marketing Synchronization: campaign fit, funnel alignment, media strategy, audience adaptation.
- Operational Embodiment: delivery fit, customer experience, internal behavior, service reality.
- Behavioral Branding: habit design, decision influence, interaction pattern, reinforcement loop.
- Audience Perception: memorability, interpretation, relevance, belief creation.
- Authority Positioning: expertise signal, category leadership, proof system, confidence.
- Market Differentiation: distinctness, category tension, competitor separation, value logic.

## Failure Detection

Scores below threshold create recommendations and governance flags.

## Use

The scoring engine should run after synthesis and before client delivery. It can also run against audits, presentations, campaign systems, identity systems, and governance reviews.
