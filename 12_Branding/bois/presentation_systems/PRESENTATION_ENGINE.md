# Presentation Engine

Client presentations must be generated as strategic decision systems, not decorative decks.

## Required Sections

- market analysis
- sector analysis
- audience psychology
- positioning rationale
- emotional territory
- symbolic reasoning
- typography reasoning
- color reasoning
- narrative systems
- campaign logic
- governance logic
- implementation roadmap

## Quality Standard

The presentation must feel:

- engineered
- psychologically informed
- culturally aligned
- strategically rigorous
- market-specific

## Delivery Logic

Every slide should connect one of these:

- market reality
- audience cognition
- category tension
- brand logic
- identity expression
- production path
- governance control
- business outcome
