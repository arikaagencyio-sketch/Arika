## Phase 3 — Backbone Architecture (synthesized)

**Core systems:** Weekly Advisory Cadence System, GTM Diagnostic System, Revenue Architecture Review System, Scale Planning System, Executive Decision-Support System.

**Immutable components:** weekly cadence is non-negotiable (the offer's value proposition is consistent access, not project check-ins); every advisory session produces a written decision-log entry (paper trail for both client value and agency protection); **no implementation work performed by the advisory team itself** — an explicit anti-scope-creep boundary not seen in any other offer (advisory recommends; execution is delivered by other offers/teams).

**Customization components:** advisory focus area (GTM vs. org-design vs. fundraising-readiness vs. M&A), cadence depth (weekly vs. bi-weekly), VIP-day usage frequency, which executives are involved.

## Phase 4 — Client Journey (synthesized, condensed)
