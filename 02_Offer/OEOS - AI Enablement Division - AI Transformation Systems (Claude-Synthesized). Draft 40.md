## Phase 3 — Backbone Architecture (synthesized)

**Core systems:** AI Readiness Assessment System, AI Roadmap & Strategy System, Change Management & Training System, AI Governance/Risk System, Workflow Implementation System (shared with/handed off to offer #6), Adoption Measurement System.

**Immutable components:** readiness assessment required before any roadmap is built (data quality, team AI-literacy, process maturity); a governance/risk framework (model risk, data privacy, bias/fairness) required before any production AI deployment — **the most rigorous compliance immutable of any offer**, appropriate to its "Very High" complexity rating; training and change management included as core, not optional — the feature that distinguishes this offer from offer #6's build-only scope.

**Customization components:** transformation scope (single department vs. enterprise-wide), AI maturity starting point, industry-specific AI governance requirements (e.g. healthcare/finance verticals need materially more rigorous governance), build-vs-buy decisions for underlying AI tooling.

## Phase 9 — Client Constraint System (synthesized)
