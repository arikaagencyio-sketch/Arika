## Phase 3 — Backbone Architecture (synthesized)

**Core systems:** Partner Sourcing & Outreach System, Joint Venture Structuring System, Affiliate/Referral Infrastructure System, Partnership Management & CRM System, Performance Tracking System.

**Immutable components:** written partnership agreement required before any referral activity begins (no informal handshake deals); performance tracking/attribution required for every partner relationship (mirrors the attribution-first immutable already established in offer #4); quarterly partner-relationship review cadence.

**Customization components:** partnership-type mix (affiliate vs. JV vs. referral vs. strategic intro), commission/incentive structure, partner-tier system depth, industry-specific partnership norms.

## Phase 4 — Client Journey (synthesized, condensed)

Standard 17-stage journey, **Day 1-120 — the longest stated timeline of any offer captured so far.** Partnership relationships inherently take longer to mature than infrastructure builds; this is a real structural characteristic of the offer, not a delivery weakness.
