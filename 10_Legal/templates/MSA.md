# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

## 1. Structure of this Agreement

1.1 This Agreement sets the terms governing all Services. It does not itself commit either
Party to any Services.

1.2 Services are engaged under a **Statement of Work** ("**SOW**") signed by both Parties. Each
SOW incorporates this Agreement.

1.3 **Order of precedence**, on conflict: (a) the **DPA**; (b) the applicable **SOW**; (c) this
Agreement; (d) any other document. *A SOW may vary this Agreement only where it says expressly
that it is doing so, identifying the clause varied.*

1.4 If no SOW is in force, this Agreement remains in effect but no Services are owed.

## 2. Services

2.1 Arika will perform the services described in each SOW (the "**Services**") with reasonable
skill and care.

2.2 **Only what the SOW says.** Anything not expressly stated in a SOW is out of scope,
including work a Party may consider implied or customary.

2.3 **Change control.** A change to scope, fees, or timeline takes effect only when recorded in
writing and signed by both Parties. **Work performed outside a signed SOW or change order is
not chargeable and not owed.**

2.4 **Client dependencies.** Arika's obligations are conditional on the Client providing access,
data, decisions, and approvals identified in the SOW, within the timeframes stated. Where the
Client does not, Arika's timeline extends by the period of delay and Arika is not liable for the
consequences.

2.5 **No exclusivity.** Nothing here prevents Arika serving other clients, including in the
Client's industry, subject to Clause 6 and the SOW's conflict provisions (if any).

## 4. Term and termination

4.1 This Agreement runs from the Effective Date until terminated.

4.2 **For convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement on `[30]` days' written
notice, provided no SOW is in force. A SOW may be terminated for convenience only where it says
so.

4.3 **For cause.** Either Party may terminate immediately on written notice if the other:
(a) commits a material breach not remedied within `[14]` days of written notice; or
(b) becomes insolvent, enters administration, or ceases to trade.

4.4 **Non-payment.** Arika may terminate a SOW immediately where fees are overdue by more than
`[NUMBER]` days.

4.5 **On termination:** the Client pays for Services performed and expenses incurred up to the
effective date; each Party returns or destroys the other's Confidential Information (Clause 6.5);
and Clauses 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 11 and 13 survive.

4.6 **Handover.** On termination for any reason other than the Client's material breach, Arika
will, if the Client asks within `[30]` days, deliver Deliverables completed and paid for, in the
formats stated in the SOW. **Handover beyond that is chargeable at Arika's then-current rates.**

## 5. Intellectual property

5.1 **Pre-existing IP stays with its owner.** Neither Party acquires rights in the other's
Background IP.

5.2 **Arika Background IP** includes its methods, frameworks, templates, agent systems,
operating documentation, tooling, and anything developed outside or before this engagement —
whether or not used in the Services.

5.3 **Deliverables.** On **full payment** of all sums due under the relevant SOW, Arika assigns
to the Client all right, title and interest in the **Deliverables** identified in that SOW,
excluding Arika Background IP embedded in them.

> ⚠️ **Read 5.3 carefully.** Assignment is conditional on **payment in full**. Until then the
> Client has **no ownership** of the Deliverables. Whether that is enforceable as drafted under
> Kenyan law, and whether it is commercially acceptable to your clients, are **counsel and
> owner questions**.

5.4 **Licence to Arika Background IP.** Where a Deliverable contains Arika Background IP, Arika
grants the Client a **non-exclusive, perpetual, worldwide, non-transferable** licence to use it
**solely as part of that Deliverable** and solely for the Client's internal business purposes.
It does not permit extraction, resale, or licensing of the Background IP on its own.

5.5 **Client Materials.** The Client grants Arika a licence to use Client Materials (data, brand
assets, content) solely to perform the Services. The Client warrants it has the rights to grant
it.

5.6 **AI-generated material.** Deliverables may include material generated with AI tools. See
[`IP_COPYRIGHT_TRADEMARK_TERMS.md`](IP_COPYRIGHT_TRADEMARK_TERMS.md) — **its §4 flags a real,
unresolved question about the copyright status of AI-generated output, which Clause 5.3 cannot
assign away.** This is the single most under-settled point in this Agreement.

5.7 **Residual knowledge.** Nothing prevents Arika using general skills, know-how, and
experience retained in the unaided memory of its personnel, provided no Confidential Information
is disclosed and no Client IP is used.

## 6. Confidentiality

6.1 "**Confidential Information**" means non-public information disclosed by one Party to the
other, in any form, that is marked confidential or would reasonably be understood as such.

6.2 The receiving Party will keep it confidential, use it only for this Agreement, and disclose
it only to personnel and sub-processors who need it and are bound by equivalent obligations.

6.3 **Exclusions:** information that is or becomes public other than by breach; was already
lawfully held; is independently developed without reference to it; or is received from a third
party without restriction.

6.4 **Compelled disclosure.** Permitted where legally required, with prompt written notice to the
other Party where lawful, and disclosure limited to what is required.

6.5 **Return or destruction.** On termination or request, the receiving Party returns or destroys
Confidential Information, except copies required by law or held in routine backups, which remain
subject to this Clause.

6.6 **Duration.** These obligations run for `[3]` years from disclosure, and **indefinitely for
trade secrets and personal data**.

6.7 A separate NDA, if signed, supplements this Clause; on conflict, **the more protective term
applies**.

fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation, or anything else that cannot lawfully be limited.

9.3 **Cap.** Each Party's total aggregate liability arising out of or in connection with this
Agreement and all SOWs is limited to **`[the total fees paid by the Client under the relevant
SOW in the [12] months preceding the claim]`**.

> ⚠️ **Three real questions for counsel and owner, not one.**
> 1. **Is the cap enforceable as drafted under Kenyan law?**
> 2. **Is it commercially survivable?** Arika's Starter audit is **$2,500** (`02_Offer`). A cap
>    at fees-paid means a **$2,500 engagement caps liability at $2,500** — while the client acts

> 3. **Does `[ARIKA LEGAL ENTITY]` sit behind this cap?** A **sole proprietorship has no
>    liability shield** — the cap is contractual only, and personal assets sit behind it. See
>    counsel question 7 in `LEGAL_RESEARCH.md` §6. **Resolve the entity before signing anything
>    with a liability cap.**

9.4 The exclusions and cap apply regardless of the basis of the claim.

9.5 Each Party will take reasonable steps to mitigate its loss.
