  * cybersecurity,
  * operational continuity.

The most important layer.

  * projects with asymmetrical return,

  * scalable economics,
  * expanding moats.

  * return on incremental capital exceeds cost of capital significantly.



**4\. Optionality Capital**

Used for:

  * acquisitions,
  * distressed opportunities,
  * downturn deployment,
  * market dislocations.



Most firms are forced sellers during crises.
