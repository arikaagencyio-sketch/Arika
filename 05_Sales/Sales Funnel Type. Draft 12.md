Here are the main ones you’ll see:

**1\. Basic Sales Funnel (3 stages)**

  * **Top of Funnel (TOFU)** – Awareness (getting attention) 
  * **Middle of Funnel (MOFU)** – Consideration (nurturing leads) 
  * **Bottom of Funnel (BOFU)** – Conversion (closing the sale) 



This is the simplest and most common structure.

**2\. AIDA Funnel**

Based on the classic AIDA model:

  * Attention 
  * Interest 
  * Desire 
  * Action 



Very popular in marketing and copywriting.

**3\. Extended Funnel (5–7 stages)**

A more detailed version:

  * Awareness 
  * Interest 
  * Consideration 
  * Intent 
  * Evaluation 
  * Purchase 
  * Loyalty/Retention 



Used in more complex sales processes.

**4\. Flywheel Model (Modern alternative)**

Popularized by HubSpot:

  * Attract 
  * Engage 
  * Delight 



Focuses on customer experience and repeat business rather than a linear funnel.

**5\. B2B Sales Funnel**

Often longer and relationship-driven:

  * Lead generation 
  * Qualification 
  * Demo/meeting 
  * Proposal 
  * Negotiation 
  * Closing
