**Layer 2 — Value-Based (Professional Tier)**

**Goal:** Justify price through outcomes

**Buyer Psychology:**

**Sales Mechanism:**

  * Case studies 
  * ROI framing 
  * Objection handling 



**Delivery:**

  * Semi-custom 
  * Structured onboarding 



**Layer 3 — Strategic / Solution Tier**

**Goal:** Solve a meaningful business problem

**Sales Cycle:**

  * Longer (weeks → months) 



**Buyer Psychology:**

  * Risk-aware 
  * Stakeholder-involved 



**Sales Mechanism:**

  * Discovery-led selling 
  * Diagnosis before pitch 
  * Relationship building 



**Delivery:**

  * Customized

**Layer 4 — Premium / Authority Tier**

**Goal:** Sell expertise, positioning, and access

**Buyer Psychology:**
