**STAGE 7 — CONTENT SYSTEM DESIGN**

**360° Growth Revenue Agency Content Architecture Blueprint**

Based on the system we have been constructing, we are no longer designing a content marketing system.

We are designing a **Revenue Influence System**.

The distinction is important.

Most agencies build content systems around:

- Platforms

- Formats

- Topics

- Publishing schedules

The 360° Agency must build content around:

**Business Problems → Strategic Intelligence → Decision Influence → Demand Creation → Revenue Generation**

This means the content system is not a publishing machine.

It is an intelligence infrastructure.

**1. Build Market Authority**

**4. Support Sales**

Provide educational and operational resources.

Create assets that become increasingly valuable over time.
