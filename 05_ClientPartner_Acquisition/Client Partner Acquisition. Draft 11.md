**1.2 Pipeline Intent (How acquisition becomes predictable)**

The pipeline is engineered as a **4-stage conversion system** :

**Stage 1 — Signal Capture**

  * Identify ICP-aligned signals:
    * hiring activity
    * funding/news expansion
    * broken funnels / weak marketing systems
    * scaling companies
    * partnership opportunity triggers



**Stage 2 — Qualification Filtering**

  * Score leads using:
    * Revenue potential
    * urgency signals
    * authority level
    * strategic alignment (client vs partner fit)



**Stage 3 — Engagement Sequencing**

  * Multi-touch structured engagement:
    * cold outreach → value hook → insight drop → proof → CTA

**Stage 4 — Conversion Layer**

  * Convert into:
    * booked calls
    * pilot projects
    * retainer agreements
    * strategic partnerships
